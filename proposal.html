Alice's Project Proposal

What is your website about?

My website will be a site dedicated to informing Boston tourists and locals of my reviews on the best restaurants to try in the Boston Area. The home page will introduce the different Boston districts and under that there will be a table that cohesively ranks all the reviewed restaurants into one list. The table can sort by low to high ratings, high to low ratings, by districts, and cuisine. Each page will be of a different district (North End, Chinatown, Allston, etc) of restaurants.

What is the objective of your website?

I think a Boston restaurant review page is useful because as a college student who is not from this area, I struggle with finding restaurants to eat at, especially during my first year. I constantly found myself going back to the same places because I was too afraid to try new places that may disappoint me. As I am entering my 4th year here, I believe that I have found some of the best of the best. And as a food enthusiast, I am always on the search for new restaurants to try. There is always Yelp, but I have found throughout the years that the reviews on there doesn't go that much in depth. This page will be able to provide some first hand dining experiences.

Who is your target audience?

My target audience are college students in the Boston Area who are not familiar with the area, but wants to eat delicious food. Specifically, students who like to eat Asian food because the majority of the featured restaurants will be Asian. However, there will be a couple non Asian restaurants.

What content do you plan to include in each page of your website?

The homepage will include an introduction of myself to build credibility for the page. There will also be an introduction of Boston and the different districts in Boston. These districts will then redirect the user to a second page, depending on the district name. Additionally, I would like to add a table that lists all the restaurants that are discussed and ranked from high to low.

Each page will include an introduction of each district and a little bit about the demographic. This is because the demographic of the area does affect what food the area produces. After the introduction, the restaurants will be introduced. Each review per restaurant will include a highlight dish, the dining experience, the address, and price range. This will be repeated on each district's page.
